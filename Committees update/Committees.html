The presentation committee’s core objective is to represent enactus Assiut in enactus' dedicated competitions and perform presentations whenever and wherever the need arises. Presentations are often dedicated to highlighting some project examples that have been worked on or sustained over the year. Members use their presentation, script writing, and English skills to deliver a powerful descriptive presentation that can meet enactus’ criteria of judging at the competition.

Enactus Assiut holds many events, most of which are on a rather large scale, and can prove tedious to hold. This is where the Organization Committee comes in, it’s a committee responsible for event planning and organization for enactus Assiut and its partners or sponsors. They oversee everything from the event details, schedule and activities all the way to holding the event and making sure it goes as planned.

The PR Committee is the committee that represents enactus when it comes to starting partnerships and/or business deals, or preparing sponsorship proposals. Like in any organization, the PR is the committee that fronts dealing with other entities and handles the public wherever applicable, be it in public events that are not hosted by enactus or on social media platforms. In enactus Assiut, the PR committee divides into three main teams. Partnerships PR, Sponsorships and business PR, and Mass Media PR.

The Fundraising committee not only acts as the financial department for enactus Assiut, it’s also the main asset for supplying the organization and other committees with money to facilitate their work and ensure other enterprises are financially supported. Additionally, the fundraising committee talks business with potential partners or sponsors and has the final say in sealing any deal that involves financial assets.

The HR committee simulates the traditional HR role in any organization. It’s tasked mainly with maintaining the organization’s structural health and integrity, alongside keeping track of the members’ performance through systematic evaluations. HR members work closely with all committees and are present in any and all activities regarding enactus where they collect data that helps them evaluate performance of teams and stay up to date on the workflow of the organization and its general direction.

The Media committee are the curators of enactus Assiut’s digital presence. It’s the committee responsible for producing any digital content related to enactus Assiut, from shooting and editing videos and pictures to publishing them on various platforms and social media. Subcommittees include Photography, Videography, Design, Social Media, and IT.

The Project committee is a pivotal one, since it performs most of the actual work in real life. It’s responsible for implementing the idea for the project that enactus Assiut is working on for the year. They conduct social and market surveys, collect and analyze the data, and decide on the best approach to execute the project, then implement it.

The marketing committee analyzes the market for segmentation and possible business endeavors. Additionally, it manages the marketing campaigns for Enactus’ projects and any events that are organized or hosted by enactus in Assiut.
